## Worker Focus
Sibling recipes use consistent terminology, comparable section depth, and consistent frontmatter conventions (tags, platforms, author format). Cross-references use correct scope identifiers.

## Verify
Terminology consistent across siblings; section depth comparable or disparity explained; frontmatter conventions aligned; cross-references valid.
