# Puppy Naming Team

## Role

Collaborative puppy name selection through breed expertise, lifestyle assessment, and temperament analysis.

## Specialists

- **breed** — Breed characteristics, lineage, physical traits, naming traditions, and cultural associations.
- **lifestyle** — Owner lifestyle, living situation, activity level, and how they match to different breeds and names.
- **temperament** — Energy levels, sociability, trainability, and personality-driven name suggestions.

Each specialist dispatches specialities for focused analysis. See `specialists/<name>/specialist.md`.
